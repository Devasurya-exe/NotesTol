HardDisk

HardDisk

1.Hard disk is the most common storage device in the computer system.
2.It is also known as Harddrive, fixed disk drive etc.
3.It is used to store data and program permenant manner ,that is it does not get erased even when the computeris switched off.
4.This device is also known as hard disk because it's hard, rigid and made eith aluminium or glass-ceramic materials(disk platter).
5.Information is stored in the hard disk by using a maganetic recording method.

Harddisk Components

1.Disk Platter
2.Read/Write Head
3.Head Arm/ Head Slider
4.Head activator Mechanism
5.Spindle Motor
6.Logic Board
7.Air Filter
8.Bezel
9.Cable and Connector

Disk Platter

Most common size - 3 and a half inch
Portable Computer Platter size - 2 and a half
Latest small platter - 1 and a half inch

Available in 10MB or more
Number of platter can be from 1 to 10 (Common number used is 2 or 3 )
Made from alluminium alloy for the light weight,But alluminium metal is not thermally stable
Leading to use glass/glass-ceramic compound.
-more storage,small size
Material used for platter is called Substrate.

Two types of coating are ther for coating the disk platter
1.Iron Oxide
2.Thin film media

Iron oxide

Iron oxide componet is used for the coating.
30 micron inch size.
Not possible for high density storage.
Crashes can easily occur if scracthes are present.
Tradition method of coating.

Thin Film Media

Thickness is 1 to 4 micron inches.
Very hard and perfect and so can withstand crashes
Two methods to achieve this coating are:
1.Platting process
2.Sputtering process

Platting Process


The disk plate is immersed in diffent chemicals

Sputtering Process


1st coated with nickel phosphorus
2nd 1 to 2 micron inch of cobalt alloy material is deposited
3rd Carbon is coated for protection
This coating helps to survive Head crashes.
Cost of coating is very high.
